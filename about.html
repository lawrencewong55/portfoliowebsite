General Assembly - Front End Web Development

When I was going through my career change, I had enrolled into a 6-week course in Front-end Web Development at General Assembly in London. From there, I had learned the basics of HTML as well as CSS and Javascript, to make the pages more interactive.


My time at General Assembly was fun and it is all thanks to my tutors that were informative and helpful. My 6 weeks was also challenging too as each week, we were provided with lessons and then homework to test out knowledge on. The challenges were a little difficult towards the end of the term, but that was because we were introduced to Javascript.


Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my time with the Front end web development course, and now I am equipped with some new skillsets to work on my own projects and tackle on new challenges too!
